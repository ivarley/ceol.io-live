What's ceol.io?

This site allows you to track a list of traditional Irish tunes you know and play.

Its main function is to be your personal tune list. You can add tunes you want to learn, or already know, and sync your Tunebook from TheSession.org. You can then sort them so you're learning the right tunes first: the tunes people really play in your sessions.

The site also tracks basic info about sessions, like the address and the recurrence, i.e. how often it happens (like "Every Thursday"). Sessions can be linked to a session record on TheSession.org, and we can import the details from there.

Each time you hold a session, you can log the tunes you played on this site. Logs have a date, a list of tunes, and a little other info.

To add a session log, click on "Add" on the Session details page, and it'll ask for the date (defaulting to the next scheduled one based on your session recurrence).

You can then add tunes by typing or pasting the tune names into the text box on the page. It'll automatically link any tunes it can; right click any tune names to see your options on editing, relinking, moving, etc.

You can think of this site as a sort of companion to TheSession.org. Info about tunes and sessions all over the world is there. This site adds the social component of tracking what tunes are really played where.

If you'd like to add your session to our list, feel free!

Key Features

  • Personal Tune List - Track tunes you know, are learning, or want to learn
  • Session Logs - Record what tunes were played at each session
  • Tune Popularity - See which tunes are most commonly played at sessions
  • TheSession.org Integration - Import tunes from your tunebook and link to tune details
  • Mobile-Friendly - Use it at the session on your phone
